Honda ADV 160 2026 Specifications & Review

This site plan outlines the homepage layout, content, and visual design for the Honda ADV 160 2026 adventure scooter. It includes the hero section, key features, color scheme, typography, and wireframes for both desktop and mobile views.

Site Name

Honda ADV 160 2026 Specifications & Review

Site Purpose

This website provides detailed information about the Honda ADV 160 2026, including its unique features, specifications, and overall riding experience and performance.

Scenarios (User Questions)

  • Why should I buy the Honda ADV 160 2026?
  • What are the specifications of the Honda ADV 160 2026?
  • How do I know if this scooter fits my riding style?

Color Schema

  • Red (#CC0000) — Headings and Navigation Bar
  • Black (#000000) — Main Body Text
  • Gray (#808080) — Subheadings, minor accents, footer text
  • White (#FFFFFF) — Background and text on red headings/nav

Typography

  • Heading Font: Oswald — used for headings and navigation bar
  • Body Font: Roboto — used for paragraph text and descriptions
